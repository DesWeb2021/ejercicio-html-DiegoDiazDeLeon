Audi A5

The Audi A5 Coupé is characterized by its sporty and elegant design. Now it has lines that accentuate the depth and breadth. There are three versions available: Select, S line and S line quattro. It features Audi Connect services: Audi Connect: Car2X Safety & Service and Infotainment, MMI plus Radio with 10.1-inch touchscreen, Audi phone box, Audi smartphone interface and optional Bang & Olufsen Sound System with 3D sound *.
With a sophisticated interior design, the new Audi A5 Coupé will surprise you with the new center console and its spacious interior.
Headlights with LED technology are now standard on the Audi A5 Coupé. Matrix LED headlights with Audi laser light are available as an option. Audi laser light complements the LED high beam at high vehicle speeds and provides significantly more safety and comfort than LED high beam by nearly doubling the visual range.
