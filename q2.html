Audi Q2

The urban SUV features an impressive and powerful design, as well as a wide range of information, entertainment and technology options. Highlights such as: progressive steering and 8-speed automatic transmission guarantee comfort and great driving fun. The new Audi Q2 is the perfect SUV for city, highway and weekend getaways.
Surprisingly full of character: the new front and rear bumper design, dynamic body lines, five new exterior colors, new LED headlights, three new side blade colors and five new wheel designs give the new Audi Q2 a powerful and unique look.
The new Audi Q2 guarantees endless driving fun thanks to its 1.4 TFSI engine with 8-speed automatic transmission, optional sports suspension and progressive steering as standard. Get even more feeling of comfort on your journeys, as the new Audi Q2 features Audi drive select, which allows you to choose between different driving modes, making every kilometer an unforgettable experience.
